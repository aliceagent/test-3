## Sages & Commentators (贤者与注释家)

### Hillel (希勒尔 / הלל)
**Era:** ~110 BCE – 10 CE | **Chinese Period:** Han Dynasty (汉朝)
- President of the Sanhedrin, known for kindness and patience
- Articles:
  - [What is a Dvar Torah](/weekly-parsha/001-what-is-dvar-torah/) — Quote from Pirkei Avot 2:4

### Rashi (拉什 / רש"י)
**Era:** 1040-1105 CE | **Chinese Period:** Song Dynasty (宋朝)
- Rabbi Shlomo Yitzchaki, most famous Torah commentator
- Articles:
  - [Bereishit: Why God Created the World](/weekly-parsha/002-bereishit-creation/) — Commentary on Genesis 1:1
  - [What is a Dvar Torah](/weekly-parsha/001-what-is-dvar-torah/) — Referenced as classical commentator

### Rabbi Moshe ben Machir (拉比摩西本马基尔 / רבי משה בן מאכיר)
**Era:** 16th century CE | **Chinese Period:** Ming Dynasty (明朝)
- Author of *Seder haYom*, first recorded source of Modeh Ani prayer
- Articles:
  - [Modeh Ani: The First Words a Jew Says Every Morning](/prayer/028-modeh-ani/) — Creator of the prayer text

### Rabbi Noson Weisz
**Era:** Contemporary
- Aish HaTorah scholar
- Articles:
  - [Bereishit: Why God Created the World](/weekly-parsha/002-bereishit-creation/) — Concept of perfectible world

### Rabbi E.E. Dessler (德斯勒拉比)
**Era:** 1892-1953 CE | **Chinese Period:** Republic of China / Early PRC
- Author of Michtav M'Eliyahu
- Articles:
  - [The Binding of Isaac](/torah-study/003-binding-of-isaac/) — Analysis of Abraham's test
