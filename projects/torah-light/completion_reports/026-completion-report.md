## Key Content Covered

### Major Certification Agencies
- **OU (Ⓤ)** - Orthodox Union, world's largest (1.3M+ products, 105 countries)
- **OK (Ⓚ)** - OK Kosher, clear symbol restriction system
- **Star-K (★-K)** - Stricter standards, Cholov Yisroel milk requirements
- **Kof-K (🗝-K)** - One of the "Big Four" agencies

### Symbol Restrictions Explained
- **Pareve** - Neutral (neither meat nor dairy)
- **D** - Dairy
- **DE** - Dairy Equipment (more flexible)
- **M** - Meat
- **P** - Passover
- **CY** - Cholov Yisroel (Jewish-supervised dairy)
- **PY** - Pas Yisroel (Jewish-baked goods)
- **Glatt** - Extra-strict meat standard

### Critical Warnings Included
- Plain "K" has no legal protection - not reliable
- Real-world example from Aish.com about Florida bakery
- Advice to use kosherquest.org for verification

### Chinese Cultural Parallels
- QS quality safety marks (质量安全)
- CCC certification (3C认证)
- Imperial food tasters (御膳房试食者)
- Red official seals (印章) as authentication
- Historical connection to Late Shang Dynasty (商朝晚期)
