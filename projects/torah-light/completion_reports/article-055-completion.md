3. **Tu BiShvat Seder**: 16th-century Safed Kabbalistic ritual, four worlds framework
4. **Four Cups**: White to red wine progression mirroring seasons and spiritual ascent
5. **Three Fruit Categories**: Shells, pits, fully edible (kelipah, nogah, accessibility)
6. **Spiritual Lessons**: Humans as trees, rectifying Adam's sin, environmental responsibility
7. **Practical Guide**: How to conduct a seder at home with minimal resources

- **Qingming Festival** (清明节) — Tree planting and ancestral remembrance
- **Spring Festival** (春节) — Seasonal celebration with symbolic foods
- **Tree Symbolism**: Pine (longevity), Bamboo (resilience), Plum Blossom (hope), Peach (immortality)
- **Historical Period**: Later Han Dynasty (后汉, 25-220 CE) parallel to Mishnaic period
