# Aish.com: How to Convert to Judaism

**URL**: https://aish.com/how-to-convert-to-judaism/
**Author**: Kylie Ora Lobell

## Key Points

### Who Can Become Jewish?

Two ways only:
1. Born to a Jewish mother
2. Authentic Jewish conversion

### Initial Considerations Before Converting

**Life transformation questions:**
- Will completely transform your life
- May affect relationships with family and friends
- If married/have children, must consider their needs
- **Cannot practice any other religion** once Jewish
- Once you convert, you are Jewish "always and forever"
- **Fully obligated to observe ALL 613 commandments**

### The Conversion Process Steps

1. **Find a sponsoring rabbi** in your community
   - Will guide you through the process
   - Connects you with appropriate beit din

2. **Connect with a beit din** (rabbinical court)
   - Orthodox court of three rabbis
   - They determine your readiness

3. **Study with a teacher**
   - Read specific books about Judaism fundamentals
   - Curriculum varies by beit din
   - Learn what it takes to live as observant Jew

4. **Move to Jewish community**
   - MUST live within walking distance of synagogue (Shabbat requirement)
   - Need to be near kosher grocery stores/restaurants
   - Receive Shabbat and holiday invitations
   - Ability to host others
   - Be part of community

5. **Become Shabbat observant**
   - Main part of being Jewish
   - Learn and practice Shabbat laws

6. **Start keeping kosher**
   - Essential requirement
   - Must have access to kosher food sources

7. **Choose Hebrew name**
   - Will be "ben Avraham avinu ve-Sarah imeinu" (son of Abraham our father and Sarah our mother)
   - Or "bat Avraham avinu ve-Sarah imeinu" (daughter of...)

8. **Circumcision** (for men)
   - If uncircumcised: full brit milah
   - If already circumcised: hatafat dam brit (symbolic drawing of blood)

9. **Conversion test and mikveh**
   - Rabbi and beit din determine readiness
   - Test on Jewish knowledge and laws
   - Immersion in mikveh (ritual bath)
   - **After mikveh, you are officially Jewish**

### Timeline

- **Minimum: 1 year**
- Could take longer depending on:
  - Learning pace
  - Becoming observant
  - Beit din's assessment

### Important Note: Conversion Is Not Required

- Judaism believes non-Jews can have meaningful relationship with God through **Seven Noahide Laws**
- Conversion is NOT a necessity for spirituality
- Only convert if:
  - Passionate about becoming Jewish
  - Ready to embrace ALL responsibilities
  - Understand it's transformative (like receiving a new soul)
