# Homemade Chinese Food - Aish.com

**URL**: https://aish.com/homemade-chinese-food/
**Author**: Elizabeth Kurtz
**Date**: April 1, 2025

## Summary
Article about making Chinese food at home for families. Emphasizes saving money and serving nutritious meals that kids enjoy.

## Key Points
- One thing kids all eat is Chinese food
- Why wait to go out when you can make it at home?
- Save money while serving delicious meals
- Recipes are easy, Asian-inspired, and popular
- Serve with roasted broccoli/cauliflower and rice
- Leftovers reheat well

## Recipes Included
1. **Wonton Soup** - Ground beef wontons with chicken broth, ginger, garlic
2. **Asian Garlic Ginger Chicken** - Chicken thighs braised in soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar
3. **Asian Chicken Lettuce Wraps** - Ground chicken with water chestnuts, hoisin sauce
4. **Sesame Chicken** - Breaded chicken with sweet sesame sauce

## Kosher Ingredients Highlighted
- Mirin (Japanese rice wine) - available at kosher markets nationwide
- All recipes use kosher meat/chicken
- Soy sauce, sesame oil, hoisin sauce

## Cultural Notes
- Chinese food is universally popular with kids
- Home cooking allows for better nutrition control
- Recipes make enough for leftovers for school lunches
