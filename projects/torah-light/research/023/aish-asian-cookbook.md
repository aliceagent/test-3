## Summary
Recipes from "The Complete Asian Kosher Cookbook" featuring Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Filipino, and Indian cuisine - simplified for home cooks.

## Key Recipes Featured

### Japanese
1. **Sushi with Fish and Vegetables**
   - Nori sheets, sushi-grade fish or tofu
   - Vegetables: carrots, cucumber, green onions, avocado
   - Sushi rice (vinegared rice)
   - Served on Shabbos as elegant fish course
   - Cost-effective and impressive

2. **Teriyaki Salmon**
   - Very easy and elegant
   - Uses teriyaki sauce

### Dipping Sauces
- **English Mustard Dipping Sauce** (low-sodium alternative to soy sauce)
- **Wasabi Dipping Sauce** (mayo-based)
- **Traditional Soy Sauce and Wasabi**

## Cultural Notes
- Asian food can be kosher and elegant enough for Shabbos
- Guests are amazed at homemade sushi on Friday night
- Minimal cost, maximum pleasure
- Cookbook covers multiple Asian cuisines (Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Filipino, Indian)
- Recipes simplified for anyone to make

## Kosher Considerations
- Sushi-grade fish availability
- Tofu as alternative protein
- Nori sheets (dried seaweed) - kosher certified
- Rice vinegar, sesame oil
- Low-sodium options highlighted
