## Chinese Historical Context

The article references the Shang Dynasty (商朝, ~1600-1046 BCE) as the Chinese historical period corresponding to the giving of the Torah at Mount Sinai (~1300 BCE), and the late Qing Dynasty (清朝, 1644-1912 CE) as the period of major Jewish immigration to America in the early 1900s.
