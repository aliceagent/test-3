   - Reading the Megillah (Mikra Megillah)
   - Festive Purim meal (Seudat Purim)
   - Sending gifts to friends (Mishloach Manot)
   - Gifts to the poor (Matanot La'evyonim)

   - God's name never mentioned in Megillah
   - Divine providence through natural events

   - Esther's name meaning "hidden"

3. **The Story of Reversal:**
   - From mourning to joy
