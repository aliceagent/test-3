# 100 Essential Hebrew Words Every Jewish Learner Should Know


---

Learning a language can feel overwhelming—thousands of words, endless grammar rules. But here's the secret: a small core vocabulary gives you enormous comprehension. In Hebrew, roughly 100 essential words unlock most Jewish prayers, blessings, and Torah study basics.

This curated list includes the most important Hebrew words for Jewish life—terms you'll encounter in synagogue, Torah study, holidays, and everyday Jewish conversation. Each entry includes Hebrew script, transliteration, pronunciation guide, meaning, and Chinese translation (中文翻译) for Chinese learners.

Master these 100 words, and you'll understand the heart of Jewish Hebrew.

## How to Use This List

1. **Focus on recognition first**: Learn to recognize Hebrew script before memorizing pronunciation
2. **Use spaced repetition**: Review words multiple times over days/weeks (apps like Anki help)
3. **Practice in context**: Read prayers or Torah verses containing these words
4. **Group by theme**: Study related words together (God-words, time-words, etc.)
5. **Say them aloud**: Hearing yourself speak Hebrew builds confidence

## How to Practice These 100 Words

1. **Flashcards**: Create digital or physical flashcards (Anki, Quizlet)
2. **Read prayers**: Find these words in the Shema, Amidah, blessings
3. **Torah study**: Look for these words in Genesis (Bereishit)
4. **Speak aloud**: Practice pronunciation (use Forvo.com for audio)
5. **Daily review**: Study 10-20 words per day, review previous days
6. **Context**: Try translating simple Hebrew sentences using these words

**Bonus tip**: Combine with article 495 (alphabet) and 498 (vowels) to sound out Hebrew texts confidently!
