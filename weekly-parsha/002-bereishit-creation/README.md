## Key Themes

1. **Continuous Creation**: God sustains the world moment-by-moment, not a "wind-up clock" universe
2. **Torah as Blueprint**: The world was created according to the wisdom of Torah
3. **Perfectible World**: God deliberately created an imperfect world so humans could partner in perfecting it
4. **Free Will**: Human choice is sacred and has cosmic significance
5. **Tikkun Olam**: Active partnership in repairing and elevating the world

## Cultural Bridges
